Welcome, Delegates!

It is our pleasure to welcome you to the First Edition of the Anáhuac Querétaro Model of the United Nations Conference. Throughout the following three days, you will have the opportunity to discuss some of the world's most relevant affairs, and touch shoulders with knowledgeable, competent, and resilient individuals. Needless to mention, the Secretariat counts on every one of you to strive for a noteworthy participation in this event.

The objective of this Model is to introduce students to a globalized environment, where they can develop abilities such as public speaking, research skills, problem-solving, and negotiation; as well as the faculty to work with individuals that do not share the same ideals. The essence of this Conference is to ensure that all participants recognize that it is only through individual actions and tolerance, that we can create a more prosperous, inclusive, and fairer world.

Our best regards,
The AQMUN Secretariat

What is MUN?

MUN stands for Model of the United Nations. It is a simulation of the UN, where students from all over the country come together to discuss and debate current issues.

Who is allowed to participate in this conference?

AQMUN is open to high school and university students from all over the country who are ambitious, curious, and outgoing. We encourage students with altruistic tendencies to join us and contribute to making the world a more inclusive, prosperous and fairer world.

Benefits

MUN aims to educate students about current events, international relations, diplomacy, and the United Nations agenda. Along with knowledge of the world we live in, participants will develop skills such as public speaking, presentation, persuasion, situation analysis, research, and critical thinking.

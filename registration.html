Delegates who wish to participate in this Conference must register in the following link. The registration fee is $350 MXN per delegate; details regarding the payment of the this fee will be provided later on. Students are expected to have studied in depth the background guide of their committees before registering.

All delegates are requiered to send a copy of their Position Paper to the Secretariat at least 24 hours before the start of the AQMUN 2023. The Secretariat will send a confirmation email to all delegates once they have been enrolled to the Conference.
